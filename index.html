About Intervene

Empathy, made into action.

Intervene is a student-led movement founded by Seher Chauhan & Riddhita Sharma at Prometheus School, Noida — built on the belief that young people protecting young people is the most powerful intervention of all.

Our Mission

To equip every student with the tools, courage, and community to recognise bullying, protect mental wellbeing, and step in — safely and early.

Our Vision

A generation of schools where no student suffers in silence — where empathy is a skill, support is a click away, and kindness is the culture.

Student-Led Impact

Designed by students, for students. Peer ambassadors run campaigns, moderate safe spaces, and turn awareness into everyday action.

Why mental health awareness matters

1 in 7 adolescents experiences a mental health condition. Most never speak up. Early awareness, language, and support change — and save — lives.

Why anti-bullying initiatives matter

Bullying affects how students learn, sleep, and see themselves. Bystanders who feel empowered to intervene can stop incidents in seconds.
